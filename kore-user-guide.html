🏪 Distributor — Shopping aur Orders

Jo maal kharidta hai — uska alag aur simple portal

🏪

Distributor

Jootai kharidne wala retailer — apna dedicated shopping portal milta hai. Koi admin cheez nahi dikhti.

✅ Distributor KYA dekh sakta hai

Dashboard, Wishlist, Catalogue (Shop), Cart, My Orders, Profile

❌ Distributor KYA nahi dekh sakta

PO, GRN, Bills, Vendors, Users, Reports, Other distributors ke orders

🛍️ Kadam-ba-Kadam — Order Kaise Karein
1
Login karein → Dashboard dekhein Aapke apne orders, credit balance, aur cart ki jaankari milegi
2
Catalogue (Shop) tab mein products dekhein Sirf AVAILABLE products dikhenge — jo abhi order ho sakte hain
3
Product chunein → Size aur quantity dalein ⚠️ Yaad rakhein: 1 carton = 24 pairs. Sirf 24 ke multiple mein order hoga (24, 48, 72...)
4
"Add to Cart" dabayein Product cart mein chala jayega — ek se zyada products add kar sakte hain
5
Cart tab → "Checkout" button dabayein System aapka credit limit check karega — agar limit mein hai to order hoga
6
My Orders mein order track karein BOOKED → PFD → RFD → OFD → RECEIVED — har step par notification milegi
⭐ Wishlist Kya Hai?
⭐

Future ke products — Wishlist mein save karein

Kuch products abhi available nahi hote — unhe WISHLIST stage mein rakha jaata hai. Inhe aap Wishlist tab mein dekh sakte hain aur cart mein daal sakte hain, lekin order sirf AVAILABLE products ka hi BOOKED hoga.

💳 Credit Limit Kya Hoti Hai?
💳
Credit Limit = Kitne rupaye tak order kar sakte hain

Admin ne aapke liye ek limit set ki hai. Agar order ka total us limit se zyada hua — order nahi hoga aur message aayega. Pehle payment karo — limit phir se available hogi.

📌 Zaroori Niyam aur Tips

Yeh niyam portal mein automatically laagu hote hain

1
1 Carton = 24 Pairs — Sirf 24 ke Multiple mein Order Hoga

Agar aap 23 pairs cart mein daalte hain — order nahi hoga. 24, 48, 72, 96... — aise hi hona chahiye. Yeh rule automatic check hota hai.

2
Credit Limit — Order karne se pehle balance check hota hai

Har distributor ka ek maximum credit limit hoti hai. Agar aapka order total us limit se zyada hua — system automatic block kar deta hai. Payment karo, limit phir free hogi.

3
AVAILABLE vs WISHLIST — Product ka Stage

AVAILABLE: Product abhi order kar sakte hain (Shop mein dikhta hai)  |  WISHLIST: Abhi nahi aaya, baad mein aayega (Wishlist mein save karo). Expectation date dikhti hai.

4
Discount Percentage — Distributor-wise Alag

Har distributor ka alag discount % hota hai jo admin ne set kiya hai. Checkout par automatically apply hota hai — manually calculate karne ki zaroorat nahi.

5
GRN se Stock Update Hoti Hai

Jab tak GRN submit nahi hoga — stock portal mein nahi badhega. Maal aa gaya to pehle GRN banaao, tabhi stock catalog mein dikhega.

6
Overdue Payment Alert — 60 aur 80 Din

Delivery ke baad bhi payment nahi ayi to: 60+ din = Yellow warning  |  80+ din = Red alert — Dashboard par dono admins ko dikhega.

7
Real-Time Notifications — Refresh ki Zaroorat Nahi

Kisi ne bhi order kiya, status change kiya, GRN submit ki — sab logon ko turant notification milti hai portal par. Browser open rahe, sab auto update hota hai.

8
Partial Fulfillment — Order ka Kuch Hissa Pehle Milega

Agar stock ka kuch hissa abhi available hai — to pehla batch dispatch hoga (PARTIAL status). Baaki baad mein aayega. Har batch ki alag history hoti hai.

🆘 Problem Aaye To Kya Karein?
🔐

Password bhool gaye?

Super Admin se contact karein — woh password reset kar denge Users tab se.

💳

Credit limit khatam ho gayi?

Admin se credit limit badhwane ke liye ya pehle outstanding payment karo.

📦

Order status nahi badal raha?

Admin / Supervisor se contact karein — woh manually status update karenge.

❌

Cart mein add nahi ho raha?

Check karein: quantity 24 ka multiple hai? Product AVAILABLE stage mein hai? Credit limit baaki hai?
